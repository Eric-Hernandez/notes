Relative positioning

Not Positioned

Positioned

We can position an element relative to its normal position.

Without additional properties, a relative element acts as a static element.

To move the element, we need to specify a horizontal (x-axis) property (left, right) and a vertical (y-axis) property (top, bottom).

In this case, we have positioned the green box relatively. Its x-axis is offset by left: 50px; (which moves it left to right) and y-axis by top: -50px; (which moves it from bottom to top).
