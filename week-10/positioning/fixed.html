Positioned

We can position an element in relation to our viewport (our browser window).

To move the element, we need to specify a horizontal (x-axis) property (left, right) and a vertical (y-axis) property (top, bottom).

In this example, #red-box and #blue-box are displayed in their normal flow, while #green-box is moved outside of its normal flow and positioned in the bottom, right corner.
